Welcome To The Luxe Spaw


Nestled in the heart of Dublin, our luxury grooming studio offers a premier experience for discerning dog owners who seek the very best for their four-legged companions. Our state-of-the-art facility combines modern aesthetics with top-notch amenities, creating an inviting and serene environment where every dog is treated with the utmost care and attention. Specializing in a comprehensive range of grooming services, from breed-specific cuts to indulgent spa treatments, our team of experienced and passionate groomers ensures that each pet leaves looking and feeling their absolute best. At our studio, we believe that grooming is not just about appearance, but also about promoting the overall health and well-being of your cherished pet, making it a haven for dog lovers in Dublin who desire nothing but excellence.

Groomers


David

Meet David, a skilled groomer with a special knack for working with larger breeds. Originally from Vancouver, Canada, David moved to Ireland five years ago and has since become a trusted name in the local dog-loving community.

David's special expertise is reactive dog grooming, where he will work with each client 1-1, and come up with a plan to promote a positive, fear-free experience for your pet. When he’s not grooming, you’ll find him spending quality time with his beloved Vizsla, Ruby.

Pam

Meet Pam, the heart and soul of The Luxe Spaw, located in the heart of Dublin. As both the salon manager and a talented groomer, Pam specializes in creating picture-perfect cuts for small toy breeds. With a passion for pampering the tiniest of pups, she ensures each one leaves the studio looking their absolute best.

When she’s not managing the salon or working her magic on clients, Pam loves nothing more than a cosy night in with her own small rescue dog, Jack, who inspires her dedication to giving every dog the special care they deserve.

Annie

Meet Annie, the bright and passionate grooming apprentice at The Luxe Spaw. From a young age, Annie has had a deep love for animals, which naturally led her to pursue a career in dog grooming.

Now, as she hones her skills at The Luxe Spaw, Annie is driven by her dedication to animal welfare, often volunteering her time to support non-profit organizations that focus on dogs in need. Her enthusiasm and commitment shine through in everything she does, making her a beloved member of the team and a future star in the grooming world.
